Samoyed Grooming Guide

Brushing:
- Normal period: 2–3 times per week
- Shedding period: daily

Tools:
- Slicker brush
- Metal comb
- Undercoat rake
- Mat splitter

Bathing:
- Once per month normally
- Use dog shampoo + conditioner
- Dry completely with blower

Important areas:
- Behind ears
- Armpits
- Neck collar area
- Groin
